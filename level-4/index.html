Puzzle Game

Directions:

  1. Put in an image link in the texbox that asks for an image URL.
  2. Put in the number of times you want the width and length to be cut (this decides the number of pieces).
  3. Press "Submit". The pieces should appear.
  4. Press "Toggle Guide".
  5. Once the faded image pops up, you can drag the pieces at the bottom into place onto the faded image. Have fun!
